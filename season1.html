Our Full Adventure

Season 1 Curriculum: 12 Weeks of Fun & Learning!

1. Intro to Basic Math

Story: The friends turn a regular day into an adventure by going on a number hunt through their neighborhood to count flowers, steps, and swings.

Activity: Creating fun number collages.

2. The Lemonade Stand

Story: With help from their kind neighbor Mr. Johnson, the friends learn the basics of business by setting up and running their very own lemonade stand.

Activity: Building a creative paper lemonade stand.

3. Money and Choices

Story: A magical map leads the friends to a fantastical toy market where they learn about needs, wants, and making smart choices with their money.

Activity: Designing a personalized DIY piggy bank.

4. Teamwork & Collaboration

Story: The friends explore an Enchanted Forest and discover that they must combine their unique strengths to find its hidden treasures.

Activity: Creating a large-scale team mural or collage.

5. Creative Problem Solving

Story: The friends become detectives for a day, following mysterious footprints and solving clues to find a missing town treasure.

Activity: Building an invention from recycled materials.

6. Eco Explore

Story: After noticing the birds in their backyard, the friends learn about nature and decide to help the environment by building a safe space for birds.

Activity: Making a bird feeder from recycled materials.

7. Basic First Aid & Safety

Story: During a fun day at the park, Benny the Bear gets a small scrape, teaching the friends the importance of safety and basic first aid.

Activity: Decorating a personal first aid kit box.

8. Healthy Habits

Story: The friends plan a special picnic and learn from Mr. Johnson about choosing healthy, energizing foods for a day of adventure and play.

Activity: Creating a personalized DIY health chart.

9. Community Helpers

Story: A tour of their town helps the friends learn about and appreciate the important jobs of people like firefighters, librarians, and mail carriers.

Activity: Making community helper role-play hats.

10. Marketplace Creations

Story: The friends decide to create a gift for a town fair, learning how products are made by creating their very own special batch of soap.

Activity: Making and decorating soap bars.

11. Emotional Expression

Story: When one of their friends feels sad, the group learns how to recognize different emotions and the importance of showing kindness and support.

Activity: Designing and writing handmade cards for others.

12. Celebration & Reflection

Story: The friends gather to look back at all their adventures, celebrating their accomplishments and how much they've learned and grown together.

Activity: Creating memory books or a group scrapbook.
